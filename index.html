What is pollution?

Pollution encompasses the release of harmful substances into our environment, threatening air, water, and soil quality. It arises from industrial emissions, vehicle exhaust, plastic waste, and chemical runoff, posing significant health risks to humans and ecosystems. Addressing pollution requires comprehensive strategies like stricter regulations, cleaner technologies, and public awareness campaigns. Mitigating its impacts is crucial for sustaining biodiversity, combating climate change, and ensuring global well-being. By promoting sustainable practices and reducing our reliance on polluting activities, we can safeguard Earth's resources and create a cleaner, healthier environment for current and future generations. Pollution demands urgent action to secure a sustainable and prosperous future.
